Who We Are

More Than Just Threads

Sheilron wasn't started to just add to the noise. Established in 2013 and fully incorporated in 2018, we are a women-empowered business built on a simple promise: Reliability.

We know the problem you face: Suppliers who promise the world and deliver late, poor-quality uniforms. We solve that with our 20+ devoted staff and industrial capacity right here in Graniteside.
