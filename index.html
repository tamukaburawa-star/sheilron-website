About Sheilron

Established in 2013 | Incorporated in 2018

Sheilron Clothing Private Limited comprises a dedicated team of professionals committed to offering quality work wear, school wear apparel, corporate clothing branding, and corporate gifting to businesses, schools, and organizations throughout Zimbabwe.

We are a women-empowered business, forged with vision and versatility, dedicated to building lasting relationships between buyer and seller through tireless dedication to product quality.

Our Mission

To supplement product excellence with outstanding services and competitive pricing, proffering outright clothing solutions to institutions throughout Zimbabwe.

Our Services

Tailor-Made Clothing

We innovate and conceptualize a range of tailor-made clothing to meet individual requirements. No order is too big or small.

Corporate Branding

A comprehensive bouquet of corporate gifting services to help you appreciate your clients and staff.
