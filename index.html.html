About Us

Sheilron Design is a professional clothing and tailoring company that provides high-quality business wear, corporate uniforms, and school wear designed to meet both professional and everyday needs. We also specialize in the production of golf T-shirts, branded and promotional T-shirts, khaki wear, tracksuits, casual wear, and a wide range of custom-made clothing. Whether you need smart office attire, durable school uniforms, comfortable sportswear, or personalized garments for events and branding, we deliver with precision, quality fabrics, and excellent craftsmanship. At Sheilron Design, we take pride in every detail to ensure our clients look confident, professional, and well-dressed for every occasion.
